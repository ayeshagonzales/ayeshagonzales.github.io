# about

About me

I am a current PhD candidate in the Physics department at Case Western Reserve University. I work in Dr. Hinzcewski's lab, where I explore how machine learning can better help us explore biophysics and vice versa.

My interests include machine learning, biophysics, mathematical physics, modeling and simulation, and data science.
